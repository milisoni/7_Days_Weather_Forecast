7 DAYS WEATHER FORECAST APPLICATION


HOW TO USE THIS APP

Using the weather forecast application is simple and straightforward. Here are a few steps to guide you on how to use the app effectively:

  1. Launch the weather forecast application on your device.
  2. Upon opening the app, you will see the current weather conditions for a predefined city.
  3. To get the weather forecast for your desired location, use the search functionality by entering the name of the city you want to check.
  4. Once you've entered the city name, the app will display the weather forecast for the next seven days.
  5. Take a look at the current weather section to see the detailed information about the weather conditions, including temperature, humidity, wind speed, and pressure.
  6. Scroll down to view the forecast for the next six days. Each day is represented by a card displaying the day of the week, an icon representing the weather conditions, and the temperature.
  7. Plan your activities or make decisions based on the weather forecast for the upcoming days.
  8. If you want to check the weather for a different location, simply use the search functionality again and enter the name of the new city.

Remember, the app provides a week-long forecast, so you can easily plan your schedule and be prepared for any weather conditions.
